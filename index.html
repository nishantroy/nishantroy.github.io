About Me

A little bit about me



My name is Nishant Roy. I am a second-year Computer Science major at the Georgia Institute of Technology. I am interested in web/mobile development as well as machine learning. I have experience with front-end development using AngularJS and HTML5 frameworks.

I have developed strong leadership, communication and interpersonal skills as a teaching assistant and a resident advisor, which make me a versatile team player.

I am a part of two research teams on campus: Grand Challenges and the e-Stadium project. As an avid environmentalist, my Grand Challenges project is focused on methods of improving the efficiency of solar panels. With the e-Stadium team, I am working on front-end development for an AngularJS based web-app.

My love for basketball has led to me being a published writer about the NBA for Trending Top Lists , a website dedicated to opinion-based lists about a wide range of topics.

My goal is to learn how to combine my interests in machine learning and web/mobile development to be able to contribute to the onset of smarter technology and its integration in our daily lives.
